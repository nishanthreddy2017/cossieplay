  • In today's fast paced world, we find many kids and young people socially awkward. There are many ways to bring them out and make them comfortable socially. One of the wide known ways is CosPlay.
  • CosPlay is portmanteau of english words Costume and Play.
  • CosPlay is a performance art in which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character.
  • The rapid growth in the number of people cosplaying as a hobby since 1990 has made the phenomenon a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan and some other parts of Asia and in the Western world.
  • Cosplay events are common features of fan conventions.
