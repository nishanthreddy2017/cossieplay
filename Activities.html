The Party

  • On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall.
  • We create custom cosplay and fancy dress costumes exclusively for this party.
  • We get about 30 people to the parties each month and make about 4 costumes per week.
  • Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17 and kids under 6 are free. Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick.
  • There is always a live DJ at the party (who takes requests) and dancing is common (but not required).
